-A discord bot notifiying my teammates about their tasks, built on Discord.js and runs on Node.js
-Tasks are organized and hosted on a Notion page
-The bot fetches data, or tasks, through the Notion api. With the help of the Discord.js library, the bot browses through channels of our team Discord server and notify each of the department of their tasks.
